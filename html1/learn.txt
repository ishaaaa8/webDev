<head></head> -> contains metadata (i.e. data about data)
    <meta charset="UTF-8"> -> indicates the character set or character encoding
                            -> utf 8 is most commonly used encoding language
    meta http-equiv="X-UA-Compatible" content="IE=edge"> 
                            -> its completely optional to use it...
                            ->it is used to specify its compatibility with microsoft edge
    <meta name="viewport" content="width=device-width, initial-scale=1.0">
                            ->viewport is the space of screen
                            ->width of device to be occupied by the page
                            ->initial scale is the amount of zoom in
    <title>Document</title> -> the title of the document
                            eg. <title>
                            Best and top engineering colleges 
                            in delhi ncr, Noida & Btech College - JIIT
                            </title>
